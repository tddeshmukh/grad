I am currently a Physics PhD student at the University of Chicago.

How did I get here? Why a PhD program? For some people, the journey to graduate school is fairly linear: discover a love for science in childhood, pursue a physics major in undergrad, participate in research opportunities across subfields, and then apply to graduate school as the natural next step in life.
